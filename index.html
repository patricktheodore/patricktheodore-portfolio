highlights-hub.

highlights-hub is an interactive application that allows the user to view highlights from recent futbol games around the globe. From the English Premier League, to the UEFA Champions League and everyting in between. The apps also intuitively saves your favourite team to storage so you can quickly see their recent games, upcoming fixtures, and place on the league table. The app makes use of jQuery, moment.js, fetching from mupltiple web API's, and bootstrap CSS frameowrk. More development coming soon!
